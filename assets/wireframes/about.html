Neil Gagne is an 19 year old artist from Ottawa, Canada. Specializing in ceramics, painting, and printmaking, his work deals mainly with erosion and abstraction. Through the use of texture and form he delivers images free of figurative renderings, favouring fragments and subtleties of the subject matter. His ceramics reflect his obsession with chance, transience and accidents both in artwork and on a domestic level. The finished product is the result of improvised wheelwork and experimental glazing processes.
